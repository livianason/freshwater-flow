Introduction...

Completed in 2018, this project showcases data analysis, visualization, and problem solving skills. The idea for this project was talked about for years before someone would have the courage and tenacity to take it on. In 2017, we were assigned our senior projects. Design a computational project involving programming, data analysis, a hypothesis and conlcusion, and submit a 20 page research paper as your final exam. Immediately, I knew this was the project I would be working on.

Over the next 6 months, over 1,000,000 data points would be analyzed by more than 1000 lines of code, and more than 7500 words would be written to describe the details of the project, data sources, statistical analyses, and conclusions. Keep reading for more information.
